What we offer

Door to door service

So to clarify, Door to Door delivery means that the freight forwarding company will pick up the shipping container from your warehouse and bring it to the port, gate it in and file the necessary paperwork and retrieve it on the other side to be brought to your consignee’s warehouse.

Transportation of cargo with temperature regime and warehousing

Refrigerated cargo transportation involves shipping goods in compliance with a certain temperature regime for any distance.The consumer properties of goods do not change and the spoilage and loss of cargo is avoided. This type of transportation is in great demand across the food, cosmetic, chemical, pharmaceutical, and agricultural industries, among others.
